Rotterdam

This here is Rotterdam home town of Edsger W. Dijkstra a bustling city of 623,652 people which is known to be the largest port city in Europe and formally of the World. The name Rotterdam came to be known way back in the 12th century when the people of the Netherlands had constructed a dam in the river Rotte hence the name Rottedam as people settled around the newly constructed dam. Its name was then formally recognised and it was granted city rights in the year 1340 when the count of Holland had granted its rights with his power. Nearly destroyed in the events of World War 2 the city of Rotterdam had to reconstruct itself and its approach after the destruction of the city was more modernised leading it to construct several sky scrapers and many new modern architechural buildings which is rare in other Dutch cities even in this time which has led to Rotterdam having a unique culture as it became more modernised.
